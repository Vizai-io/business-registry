# Enterprise Business Profiles

This directory contains profiles for organizations with comprehensive AI governance requirements.

## Enterprise Tier

The Enterprise tier is designed for large organizations that need:

- **Comprehensive verification** - Multi-stakeholder review process
- **Enhanced metadata** - Additional fields for complex organizations
- **Managed updates** - Dedicated account management
- **Change tracking** - Detailed history of all profile modifications
- **Executive reporting** - Regular reports on AI representation
- **Multi-location support** - Detailed location and subsidiary tracking
- **Custom verification** - Tailored verification processes
- **Priority support** - Direct access to VizAI team

## Who Needs Enterprise Tier?

Consider Enterprise tier if your organization:

- Has multiple subsidiaries or business units
- Operates across many countries
- Has complex compliance requirements
- Needs executive-level reporting
- Wants dedicated account management
- Has frequent updates or changes
- Requires custom verification workflows
