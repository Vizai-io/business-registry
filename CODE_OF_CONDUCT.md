# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and professional environment for everyone contributing to the VizAI Business Registry, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Providing accurate, verifiable business information
- Respecting data quality standards

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Submitting false or misleading business information
- Harassment, trolling, or insulting/derogatory comments
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Spam or promotional content disguised as legitimate submissions
- Any conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project team at hello@vizai.io. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

Project maintainers have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces, including the GitHub repository, issues, pull requests, and any other communication channels associated with the VizAI Business Registry.
